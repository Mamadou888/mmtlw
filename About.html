All about Me

My name is Assita Diallo and I have created this blog to motivate others to live a healthy lifestyle.

I was born and raised in New York City.

I am currently a sophomore at the University at Albany with an intended major in psychology and minor in neuroscience.

My career goal is to become a physical therapist.

My favorite color is white and red. I love reading books and writing (both poetry and short stories

I have lost over 25 pounds and I am still in the process of losing more and toning up.

The Reason why I started to lose weight:

"Once upon a time I was walking through a jungle and I met one of the prettiest and most fittest girl named fitness.

As soon as I spotted her I fell in love. Ever since that day I married her and as of today we are still together.

We have made 4 years together and I am very happy about it.

........Just joking.... But there is a bit of truth in this story I tell........ !

This short anecdote is the reason why I decided to lose weight and follow a healthy lifestyle

But there is more to the story and here it goes:

All my life I have been fat and my weight to me really never mattered to me until summer of 2011

Before then I never really considered how fat and unhealthy I was.

I did not care about losing weight because I thought it was stupid.

I always thought that I was too young to worry about being fat and having to lose weight.

Since I was younger my family members would always call me fat and talk about how I needed to lose weight or soon I was only going to burst.

When these jokes were being made I really did not mind because I took it as a joke, (so it basically went through one air and out the other.

At first I did not store such negative comments in my head because I knew how damaging it would be on a young mind like mine.

But because these very close family members of mind said it all the damn time It started to grow into my head.

And every time a comment about how everything I was eating was going to kill me and that I needed to stop eating all together started to bother me more and more .

All these negative comments that I tried so hard to disregard just kept on building up in my system and after awhile I really started to believe I was fat.

So what I started to do was wear big clothes to hid my fatness, which in turn I hid who I truly was.

I tried not to eat for a couple of hours but as a fat-ass *(Excuse me) I really loved food too goddam much.

Since I can remember, food has always made me feel better and it was just soo comforting to give up.

So I gave up on trying to starve and did not care about my weight

So I sort of started binge eating. This comfort that I found in food became my shield from all the hurt I was going though.

But binge wating, especially as a child is extremely dangerous and by doing that I became fatter and unhealthier.

When I went to the doctor, they continuously told my mother how overweight I was and at the rate I was going I was soon going to end up with diabetes and heart problems.

But since I was young the idea of losing weight never occurred to me. It was not until I started high-school that I figured I had to change my life.

A close friend of mine and I had planned on working out together but there would always be an excuse as to why we could not start as soon as possible.

Soon enough though I got tired of waiting and went ahead and signed up for planet fitness which was pretty cheap and amazing.

When I started working out at the gym It was very difficult because I did not know where to start.

Not only did I not know what to do, but I had very low endurance when working out.
